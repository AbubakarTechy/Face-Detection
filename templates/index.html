How to get notifications on your mobile using ntfy:

You can receive real-time notifications with snapshots directly on your mobile device for free:

  1. 1. Install the ntfy app on your iOS or Android phone.
  2. 2. Open the app, click the + button to subscribe to a topic.
  3. 3. Enter: humancapture_abubakar_9321 and subscribe.
  4. 4. Make sure ntfy Alerts is enabled below. You'll get pushed instantly when someone is identified!
